Haystack

Description

A haystack is similar to a taco salad. It is composed of beans, chips, cheese, and sour cream. Other ingredients like vegetables can be added if one desires to also.

Ingredients

  • beans
  • corn chips
  • cheese
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. Place chips on a plate and crush them with hands.
  2. Add beans over the chips.
  3. Spirnkle cheese over the beans.
  4. Add a dollop of sour cream.
  5. Mix together and enjoy.
