Fruit Salad

Description

Fruit salad is a simple and delicious food that we can make quickly and with few ingredients. We don't even need to cook anything!

Ingredients

  • strawberries
  • mango
  • banana
  • pineapple
  • blueberries

Steps

  1. Chop the bananas pineapples and mangos to the desired sizes.
  2. Place all the ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Mix and enjoy.
