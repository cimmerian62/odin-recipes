Pizza Pocket

Description

We can make a pizza pocket easily with simple ingredients. We simply mix the filling and wrap it in a tortilla before toasting it.

Ingredients

  • tortilla
  • tomato sauce
  • cheddar cheese
  • diced chicken

Steps

  1. Mix the tomato sauce, diced chicken, and cheddar cheese together in a bowl.
  2. Place the mixed ingredients together on a tortilla and wrap it like a burrito.
  3. Toast for 5 minutes and enjoy.
